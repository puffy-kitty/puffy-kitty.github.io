# 地鼠软糖 / Diglett Gummy

## body-materials

### zh

- 主体：浅咖色线。

### us

- Body: use light brown yarn.

### uk

- Body: use light brown yarn.

## body

| round | zh | us | uk |
| ---
| --- | ---
| --- |
| R1 | 环起6x | magic ring, 6 sc | magic ring, 6 dc |
| R2 | 6v | 6 inc | 6 inc |
| R3 | 6(x, v) | 6(sc, inc) | 6(dc, inc) |
| R4-10 | 18x | 18 sc | 18 dc |
| 换线 | 整体换灰色线 | Change to gray yarn for the rest | Change to grey yarn for the rest |
| R11 | 外半针：6(x, v, x) | Front loop only: 6(sc, inc, sc) | Front loop only: 6(dc, inc, dc) |
| R12 | 6(3x, v) | 6(3 sc, inc) | 6(3 dc, inc) |

## notes

### zh

- R11 和 R12 上随机选 4-5 个 x 钩成 B，形成石头。

### us

- On R11 and R12, randomly choose 4-5 sc and work them as bobble stitches to form rocks.

### uk

- On R11 and R12, randomly choose 4-5 dc and work them as bobble stitches to form rocks.

## assess:mouth

### zh

- 红色线。
- 环起3x，SL 到环上。
- 收紧线圈时不要收到最紧，保持嘴巴是平的。

### us

- Use red yarn.
- Magic ring, 3 sc, SL st to the ring.
- Do not tighten the loop completely; keep the mouth flat.

### uk

- Use red yarn.
- Magic ring, 3 dc, SL st to the ring.
- Do not tighten the loop completely; keep the mouth flat.

## abbreviations

### zh

- x = 短针，v = 加针，B = 泡泡针，SL = 引拔针。

### us

- sc = single crochet, inc = increase, bobble = bobble stitch, SL st = slip stitch.

### uk

- dc = double crochet, inc = increase, bobble = bobble stitch, SL st = slip stitch.
