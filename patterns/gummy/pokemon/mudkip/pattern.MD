## assess:cheeks

### zh

- 橘黄线，做 2 个。
- 环起6x，6v，断线。
- 从第一圈的 2x 入针，5x 出针，拉出一个锁针线圈。
- 在线圈上钩 3 次 (2ch, 倒1回钩 SL, SL到线圈上)。
- 也可以单独环起钩线圈。

### us

- Use orange-yellow yarn and make 2.
- Magic ring, 6 sc, then 6 inc. Fasten off.
- Join through 2 sc of the first round and exit after 5 sc, then pull up a chain loop.
- On the loop, work 3 times (ch 2, turn back 1 time and SL st, SL st to the loop).
- You can also make this loop separately with a magic ring.

### uk

- Use orange-yellow yarn and make 2.
- Magic ring, 6 dc, then 6 inc. Fasten off.
- Join through 2 dc of the first round and exit after 5 dc, then pull up a chain loop.
- On the loop, work 3 times (ch 2, turn back 1 time and SL st, SL st to the loop).
- You can also make this loop separately with a magic ring.

## abbreviations

### zh

- x = 短针，v = 加针，A = 减针，B = 泡泡针，ch = 锁针，SL = 引拔针。

### us

- sc = single crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, SL st = slip stitch.

### uk

- dc = double crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, SL st = slip stitch.
