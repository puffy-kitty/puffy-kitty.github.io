# 小卡比兽软糖 / Munchlax Gummy

## body-materials

### zh

- 主体：深蓝色线。

### us

- Body: use dark blue yarn.

### uk

- Body: use dark blue yarn.

### zh

- *换白色线*

### us

- *Change to white yarn*

### uk

- *Change to white yarn*

## notes

### zh

- R10 和 R12 的 B 是身体凸起位置，使用当前针目颜色。
- 填充后调整白色肚皮居中，再缝合耳朵。

### us

- The bobble stitches in R10 and R12 form the body bumps; work them in the current stitch color.
- After stuffing, center the white belly area before sewing on the ears.

### uk

- The bobble stitches in R10 and R12 form the body bumps; work them in the current stitch colour.
- After stuffing, centre the white belly area before sewing on the ears.

## abbreviations

### zh

- x = 短针，v = 加针，A = 减针，B = 泡泡针。

### us

- sc = single crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch.

### uk

- dc = double crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch.
