# 皮丘软糖 / Pichu Gummy

## body-materials

### zh

- 主体：浅黄色线。

### us

- Body: use light yellow yarn.

### uk

- Body: use light yellow yarn.

## notes

### zh

- R10 的黑色高亮做出身体花纹。
- R10 和 R12 的 B 是身体凸起位置，使用当前针目颜色。
- 缝耳朵时让黑色半圈朝向耳尖外侧。

### us

- The black highlighted stitches in R10 form the body marking.
- The bobble stitches in R10 and R12 form the body bumps; work them in the current stitch color.
- When sewing on the ears, keep the black half-round toward the outside tip.

### uk

- The black highlighted stitches in R10 form the body marking.
- The bobble stitches in R10 and R12 form the body bumps; work them in the current stitch colour.
- When sewing on the ears, keep the black half-round toward the outside tip.

## assess:tail

### zh

- 尾巴：黑色线。
- 4ch，倒2回钩 3x；翻面3x；再翻面3x；然后起立4ch，倒2回钩 2SL 和 4x。

### us

- Tail: black yarn.
- Ch 4, from the 2nd ch work 3 sc; turn, 3 sc; turn again, 3 sc; ch 4, turn back 2 stitches and work 2 SL st and 4 sc.

### uk

- Tail: black yarn.
- Ch 4, from the 2nd ch work 3 dc; turn, 3 dc; turn again, 3 dc; ch 4, turn back 2 stitches and work 2 SL st and 4 dc.

## assess:ears

### zh

- 耳朵：黄色线，钩两个。
- 3ch，倒1回钩 SL，再钩4ch。
- 倒2回钩 SL, T, F,（ch，倒1回钩 SL）, F, T。
- 织片翻面，从菱形的一角入针，用黑色线钩半圈 x；耳朵尖的位置钩 x, ch, x。

### us

- Ears: yellow yarn, make two.
- Ch 3, turn back 1 stitch and work 1 SL st, then ch 4.
- Turn back 2 stitches and work: SL st, hdc, dc, ch; turn back 1 stitch and work: SL st, dc, hdc.
- Turn the piece over. Starting from one corner of the diamond, work a half-round of sc with black yarn; at the ear tip, work sc, ch, sc.

### uk

- Ears: yellow yarn, make two.
- Ch 3, turn back 1 stitch and work 1 SL st, then ch 4.
- Turn back 2 stitches and work: SL st, htr, tr, ch; turn back 1 stitch and work: SL st, tr, htr.
- Turn the piece over. Starting from one corner of the diamond, work a half-round of dc with black yarn; at the ear tip, work dc, ch, dc.

## abbreviations

### zh

- x = 短针，v = 加针，A = 减针，B = 泡泡针，ch = 锁针，SL = 引拔针，T = 中长针，F = 长针。

### us

- sc = single crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, SL st = slip stitch, hdc = half double crochet, dc = double crochet.

### uk

- dc = double crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, SL st = slip stitch, htr = half treble crochet, tr = treble crochet.
