## notes

### zh

- 图片标题为“拉鲁拉丝软糖”；文件夹和封面图已按 Ralts 命名。
- 身体1和身体2是两种做法，头部共用；选其中一个身体版本完成即可。
- 身体1和身体2的 R10 都钩外半针。
- 身体2 R13-14 中间单独的 x 钩在上一行折返的位置。
- 触角第一个 T 可以用长针无痕起立针；中间那一针如果觉得别扭，也可以试试 (T, ch, F, T)。

### us

- The image title reads "Ralts Gummy"; the folder and cover image are named for Ralts.
- Body 1 and Body 2 are alternate versions. Use the shared head and choose one body version.
- Work R10 of both body versions in the front loop only.
- On Body 2 R13-14, work the single middle sc into the turning section from the previous row.
- For the horns, the first hdc can be an invisible standing hdc. If the center stitch feels awkward, try (hdc, ch, dc, hdc).

### uk

- The image title reads "Ralts Gummy"; the folder and cover image are named for Ralts.
- Body 1 and Body 2 are alternate versions. Use the shared head and choose one body version.
- Work R10 of both body versions in the front loop only.
- On Body 2 R13-14, work the single middle dc into the turning section from the previous row.
- For the horns, the first htr can be an invisible standing htr. If the centre stitch feels awkward, try (htr, ch, tr, htr).

## hair

### zh

- 头发按上方表格 R1-R9 完成；R8 不引拔，直接折返后钩 R9。
- 触角用红线做 2 个，完成后缝在头发两侧。

### us

- Work the hair from R1-R9 in the table above. On R8, do not slip stitch; turn directly before R9.
- Make 2 red horns and sew them to both sides of the hair.

### uk

- Work the hair from R1-R9 in the table above. On R8, do not slip stitch; turn directly before R9.
- Make 2 red horns and sew them to both sides of the hair.

## abbreviations

### zh

- x = 短针，v = 加针，A = 减针，B = 泡泡针，ch = 锁针，sl = 引拔针，T = 中长针，F = 长针。

### us

- sc = single crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, sl st = slip stitch, hdc = half double crochet, dc = double crochet.

### uk

- dc = double crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, sl st = slip stitch, htr = half treble crochet, tr = treble crochet.
