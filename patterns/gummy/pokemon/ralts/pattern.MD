## notes:body1

### zh

- 身体1和身体2是两个可选版本，主体完成后都搭配同一组头发和触角。
- R10 钩外半针，两个 B 是脚的位置。
- R12 的 4ch 做出连接桥，SL 到本圈第10针后继续钩 R13-14。
- 补钩部分从 R12 第一针重新接线，补出另一侧的两圈 13x。

### us

- Body 1 and Body 2 are alternate versions. After the body, use the same hair and horns for either version.
- Work R10 in the front loop only. The two bobble stitches form the feet.
- The ch-4 bridge in R12 connects back to stitch 10 of the same round before R13-14.
- For the extra section, rejoin in stitch 1 of R12 and work the other side for 2 rounds of 13 sc.

### uk

- Body 1 and Body 2 are alternate versions. After the body, use the same hair and horns for either version.
- Work R10 in the front loop only. The two bobble stitches form the feet.
- The ch-4 bridge in R12 connects back to stitch 10 of the same round before R13-14.
- For the extra section, rejoin in stitch 1 of R12 and work the other side for 2 rounds of 13 dc.

## notes:body2

### zh

- 身体1和身体2是两个可选版本，主体完成后都搭配同一组头发和触角。
- R10 钩外半针，两个 B 是脚的位置。
- R12 中间的 4x 是折返小片；R13-14 中间单独的 x 钩在这段折返位置上。

### us

- Work R10 in the front loop only. The two bobble stitches form the feet.
- The 4 sc in the middle of R12 forms a turned strip; on R13-14, work the single middle sc into that turned section.

## assess:horns:body1

### zh

- 触角：红色线
- 做 2 个。
- 环起5x，不引拔直接翻面；接着钩 T, v, (T, F, ch, T), v, T。
- 完成后缝在头发两侧。

### us

- Horns: red yarn.
- Make 2.
- Magic ring, 5 sc, do not slip stitch, turn directly; then work hdc, inc, (hdc, dc, ch, hdc), inc, hdc.
- Sew to both sides of the hair.

### uk

- Horns: red yarn.
- Make 2.
- Magic ring, 5 dc, do not slip stitch, turn directly; then work htr, inc, (htr, tr, ch, htr), inc, htr.
- Sew to both sides of the hair.

## abbreviations

### zh

- x = 短针，v = 加针，A = 减针，B = 泡泡针，ch = 锁针，SL = 引拔针，T = 中长针，F = 长针。

### us

- sc = single crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, SL st = slip stitch, hdc = half double crochet, dc = double crochet.

### uk

- dc = double crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, SL st = slip stitch, htr = half treble crochet, tr = treble crochet.
