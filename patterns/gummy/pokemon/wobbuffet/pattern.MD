## notes

### zh

- R15 的 B 是底部凸起的位置。
- 按成品比例填充并调整形状。

### us

- The bobble stitches in R15 form the bottom bumps.
- Stuff and shape to match the finished proportions.

### uk

- The bobble stitches in R15 form the bottom bumps.
- Stuff and shape to match the finished proportions.

## assess:tail

### zh

- 尾巴：黑色线。
- 6ch，倒2回钩 T, F, T, x, x。第一个 T 可以用无痕长针起立；不会无痕起立时，改为 7ch，倒3回钩。

### us

- Tail: black yarn.
- Ch 6, from the 2nd ch work: hdc, dc, hdc, sc, sc. The first hdc can be an invisible standing hdc; if not, ch 7 and work back from the 3rd ch.

### uk

- Tail: black yarn.
- Ch 6, from the 2nd ch work: htr, tr, htr, dc, dc. The first htr can be an invisible standing htr; if not, ch 7 and work back from the 3rd ch.

## abbreviations

### zh

- x = 短针，v = 加针，A = 减针，B = 泡泡针，ch = 锁针，T = 中长针，F = 长针。

### us

- sc = single crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, hdc = half double crochet, dc = double crochet.

### uk

- dc = double crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, htr = half treble crochet, tr = treble crochet.
