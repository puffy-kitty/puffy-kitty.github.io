## notes:johto-half

### zh

- 地区版本和钩针版本是独立选项：这里是城都乌波 + 0.5头身。
- R11 钩外半针。
- R13 的两个 B 是脚的位置，使用主体同色线。

### us

- Regional form and body version are independent choices: this version is Johto Wooper + 0.5 head-body.
- Work R11 in the front loop only.
- The two bobble stitches in R13 form the feet and use the same yarn as the body.

### uk

- Regional form and body version are independent choices: this version is Johto Wooper + 0.5 head-body.
- Work R11 in the front loop only.
- The two bobble stitches in R13 form the feet and use the same yarn as the body.

## abbreviations

### zh

- x = 短针，v = 加针，A = 减针，B = 泡泡针，ch = 锁针，SL = 引拔针。

### us

- sc = single crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, SL st = slip stitch.

### uk

- dc = double crochet, inc = increase, dec = decrease, bobble = bobble stitch, ch = chain, SL st = slip stitch.
